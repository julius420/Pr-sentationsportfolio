About

My name is Julius Holmgaard, (23 years)

I’m a starting UI/UX designer and engineer from Odense, Denmark. I am currently studying at UCL (University College Lillebælt) to become a multimedia-designer. My skill set revolves around graphic designing, web-coding, and overall design concerning the IT-genre. For many years I have been interested in the digital and graphic aspect of websites and web coding. I have some ground experience with Adobe Photoshop, which I have used for small projects for smaller websites (banners, posters, etc.)
A big part of motivation is using my creativity as an outlet for my graphic-work. My big detail orientation and personal interest among the projects I have done so far, is sure to keep a user friendly standart in my work .

This is my overview, containing my most relevant works and experiences. Welcome to the page!
